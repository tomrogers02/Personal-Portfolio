01. About Me

As an aspiring front-end developer, I am passionate about creating engaging and innovative digital experiences. My interest in web development began in 2020 when I started my own sustainable t-shirt business and was inspired to improve the web design templates offered by larger companies. Turns out, creating an auction timer can teach you quite a bit about JavaScript.

In addition to my degree in Digital Business and Information Technologies, I am continuously honing my skills in responsive design and user interactions. Currently, I am working on my first client project where I am designing and developing an extension to their website, specifically a careers and apprenticeship page.
